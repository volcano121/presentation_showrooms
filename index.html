Core Features

3D Product Models

Customers can view and interact with highly detailed 3D models of products. Features like zoom and dynamic scaling allow for a closer inspection of details, such as textures, colors, or mechanisms.

Personalized Experiences

AI-driven customization tailors the showroom based on user preferences, displaying products that fit their style, budget, or prior searches.

Virtual Reality (VR) Immersion

Customers wearing VR headsets can "walk through" the showroom, exploring products as though they were in a physical store. This is especially effective for industries like automotive, real estate, or luxury goods.
